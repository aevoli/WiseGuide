Dolphin Tour: Our most popular and favourite tour. Discover and swim with dolphins in their native ocean!


Starting in Nungwi, we travel together to beaches of  Mnemba. Here we meet the dolphins, they are well-fed, friendly, and excited to meet you. Afterwards, you can             snorkel and see the Mnemba coral reef too.                  If you’re lucky, you might see a whale!

Prison Island Tour: Learn and experience the old history and new beauty of Prison Island.




In this half-day trip, we go to Prison Island. Originally a prison, the beautiful island has now become a turtle sanctuary. Genuinely a one-of-a-kind place. If you enjoy history, nature, a 198-year-old turtle, and adventure; then this excursion is for you.

book now

Stone Town Tour: A personalised tour of Stone Town by the WiseGuide himself.  




Coming to Zanzibar without experiencing Stone Town is a mistake! An accredited UNESCO World Heritage Centre; learn about the artistic, social, and historical centre of Zanzibar from a local who has lived here his whole life.

book now

Safari Blue Trip: Join the Safari Blue crew for a day out on the seas.




Dive into the beautiful waters of Menai Bay: Home to Safari Blue, one of the best coral reefs. The experienced crew will take you to the perfect spots for snorkelling and animal sighting. From fish, dolphins, and even whales. You will never forget the sights from this excursion.
